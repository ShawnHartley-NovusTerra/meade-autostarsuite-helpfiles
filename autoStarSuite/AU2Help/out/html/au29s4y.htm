Connect to Autostar (Autostar Commands Button Group)

When this button is clicked, the software attempts to establish a serial connection with the Autostar Handbox. The handbox must be properly connected to the PC, or an error will be reported.

Once a connection is established, the software reports the status of the handbox, such as software revision level, available memory, and number of objects.

If you attempt to transfer data to and from the handbox without clicking on the connect button first, a connection is automatically attempted.

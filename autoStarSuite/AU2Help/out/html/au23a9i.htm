Operating Instructions – P/N 07047 LX200 Serial Interface Cable

The LX200 Serial Interface Cable provides access to two LX200 operations:

·Telescope Control: Use a personal computer (PC) to directly control a Meade LX200 telescope through the use of an astronomical software program with telescope control capabilities such as the optional Meade Epoch™ 2000sk Sky Software.

·Download from a PC (LX200 GPS Models): Download a new version of the Autostar II software or object information (e.g., new satellite orbital parameters) from the Meade web site (www.meade.com) through a PC to the Autostar II.

To use your LX200 GPS and Autostar II with this and other software, perform the following steps:

1.Verify that telescope and computer power are OFF.

2.Connect the serial interface adapter to the RS–232 port on your PC.

3.Connect one end of the 6-pin serial cable to the serial interface adapter, then connect the other end to the RS-232 port CLOSEST TO THE HANDBOX PORT at the base of the telescope mount.

4.Connect the Autostar II to the HBX port on the telescope computer control panel.

5.Apply power to the telescope and computer, then complete basic Autostar initialization as detailed in the Autostar instruction manual.

6.Be sure that the computer Com Port is configured to the following specifications:

·Baud Rate9600

·Data Bits8

·ParityNone

·Stop bit1

·Flow ControlNone

7.To communicate between the Autostar II and your PC, follow the instructions for the specific software you wish to use.

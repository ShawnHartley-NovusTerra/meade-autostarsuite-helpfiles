Status Bar

The status bar is displayed at the bottom of the Autostar Update window.

The left area of the status bar describes actions of menu items as you use the arrow keys to navigate through menus. This area also shows messages that describe the progress and/or success of actions of buttons or menu commands.

The right areas of the status bar indicate the following information in the advanced view:

IndicatorDescription

ModelThe model # of the handbox currently connected, or "Unknown" if no handbox is connected.

Ver.The version of the Autostar software found in the handbox if connected, or "Unknown" if no handbox is connected.

Additional status information can be found in the List View Status Bar located at the bottom of either the Library List View or the Handbox List View.

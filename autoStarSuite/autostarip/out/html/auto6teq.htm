Fix Cold Pixels Command (Process menu)

This command is used to loacte and replace isolated bright pixels that often result from cosmic ray hits or other forms of random image noise in your calibration data. When selected, you will see a dialog that allows you determine how agressively the program locates these bad pixels. In general, the lower the setting, the more aggressive the search.

When a bad pixel is found, it replace by the average of its four surrounding neighbors.

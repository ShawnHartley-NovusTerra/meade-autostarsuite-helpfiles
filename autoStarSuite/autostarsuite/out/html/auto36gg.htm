Zoom

The field of view shown by the starmap display is changed by using the Zoom dialog box. The field of view may be entered in degrees, fractions of degrees, or by selecting one of the sky coverage buttons.

Since the size of the display window is variable, the map is scaled to include the selected field of view in the smaller of the x or y directions. If you desire a precise indication of the field of view of the telescope, select the Telescope Fields item from the Options dialog box.

Port Setup (Telescope:Communications menu)

The Port Setup command allows you to specify the parameters necessary to communicate the Autostar o your serial port. You should attach your modem or telescope to one of the available serial ports that does not conflict with other devices attached to your computer,

The default configuration is 9600 baud, 8 databits, 1 stopbit, and no parity. These settings will allow you communicate with Autostar telescopes.

Communications (Telescope menu)

Autostar Suite can communicate with your telescope by a number of different methods. It can connect directly to a PC serial port with the suppled cable. It can also be configured to dial up a remotely located telescope via a dial-up modem. It can connect to a remote or local telescope via any standard TCP/IP computer network. Or it can allow you to use a virtual handbox for a locally connected or networked telescope. The sub menus allow you select your connection method.
